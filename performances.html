Said the Whale

Said the Whale is an indie rock band from Vancouver, BC that debuted in 2007. Since that time they have released 5 studio albums and won a Juno in 2011 for new group of the year.


Well known songs include:

  • Loveless
  • I love you

Hedley

This iconic band hails from Abbotsford, BC. They burst onto the scene in 2005 after their debut album was released to much acclaim. The album eventually eventually kickstarted their successful careers.


Well known songs include:

  • Perfect
  • Never Too Late
  • For The Nights I Can't Remember

Grimes

Grimes was born in Vancouver, BC. She won breakthrough artist of the year, and her album Visions won the best electronic album of the year at the Junos in 2011.


Well known songs include:

  • Genesis
  • Venus Fly
